Please complete the following two tasks and create a presentation.

## Task 1
Using the datasets in /data, map the ratio of UPRNs to the total sum of all crimes in November 2025 for Lewisham’s wards. 

## Task 2
Calculate the correlation of total crime and 2025 Indices of Multiple Deprivation in London at LSOA level for November 2025.

## Presentation instructions
In the presentation, you should:
- Detail the methodology including the applications used
- Present your results
- Argue for or against this approach to mapping crimes
- Suggest improvements

For Task 1, your presentation should show the following maps:
- Count of UPRNs per ward
- Count of crimes per ward
- Ratio of UPRNs to crimes per ward

Additionally, you should present the following tables:
- Counts of different types of crimes per ward
- Ratios of UPRNs to different types of crimes per ward

For Task 2, it is up to you how you want to present the results, but a mapping element is expected.

## Datasets provided:
- UPRN lookup dataset (November 2025): lewisham_uprn_lookup.csv
- Lewisham ward boundaries (December 2025): ward boundaries folder
- London LSOA boundaries (2021): lsoa boundaries folder
- MPS crime data at LSOA level: MPS LSOA Level Crime (most recent 24 months).csv
- MPS crime data at ward level: MPS Ward Level Crime (most recent 24 months).csv
- 2025 Indices of Multiple Deprivation: File_1_IoD2025_Index_of_Multiple_Deprivation.xlsx
